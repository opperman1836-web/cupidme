# TASK 3: LEAD CAPTURE SYSTEM

---

## OPTION A: WHATSAPP FUNNEL (PRIMARY)

### How It Works:
1. Lead sees ad → WhatsApps keyword to your number
2. You send auto-reply with course info
3. You ask qualifying questions
4. You send offer + payment details
5. Lead pays → You confirm enrollment

---

### STEP 1: Auto-Reply Message (Send immediately when someone messages)

```
Hi there! 👋 Welcome to New Skills Academy!

Thank you for your interest in our Caregiver Programme. 🏥

Here's what's included in our SPECIAL R2,000 package:

✅ Home-Based Care Training
✅ Health Care Assistant Certification
✅ FREE First Aid & Basic Life Support (worth R1,200)
✅ FREE HIV & AIDS Counselling (worth R800)
✅ FREE TB Management Module
✅ Certificate of Completion

💰 Normal Price: R5,500
🔥 Special Price: R2,000 (You save R3,500!)

⚠️ Only 30 spots left at this price!

To help you enrol, can I ask you a few quick questions?

1️⃣ What is your full name?
2️⃣ Where are you based (city/town)?
3️⃣ Are you currently employed or looking for work?

Just reply with your answers and I'll get you started! 😊
```

### STEP 2: Qualifying Questions Flow

After they answer the first 3 questions, send:

```
Thank you, [Name]! 😊

Just a few more questions to make sure we set you up perfectly:

4️⃣ How did you hear about us?
5️⃣ When would you like to start? (We have immediate / next week options)
6️⃣ Will you be paying via EFT, cash deposit, or mobile transfer?

You're almost enrolled! 🎉
```

### STEP 3: Qualification Criteria

**HOT LEAD (Close immediately):**
- Answered all questions
- Expressed urgency ("I want to start now/soon")
- Asked about payment
- Currently unemployed or actively seeking skills

**WARM LEAD (Nurture for 24-48 hours):**
- Responded but hasn't answered all questions
- Said "I'll think about it"
- Asked many questions about the course

**COLD LEAD (Follow up in 3-7 days):**
- Sent keyword but never replied to auto-message
- Read messages but didn't respond

### STEP 4: Move Lead to Payment

Once qualified, send:

```

Here are your payment details:

🏦 BANK DETAILS:

Branch Code: 470010
Reference: [THEIR NAME + PHONE NUMBER]

💰 Amount: R2,000

✅ Once you've made payment, just send me your proof of payment (screenshot or photo) and I'll confirm your enrollment immediately.

⚠️ Remember: Only 30 spots at this price — first come, first served!

Need help? Just ask — I'm here for you! 😊
```
